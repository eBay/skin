@ebay/skin/success-message

A success-message is a message of celebration used when a user has completed something important. Its higher energy than a confirmation notice and is always the major focus of the page.

It can be used within a dialog, or as a full screen in mobile applications.

With an action button

Your first order has been placed!

You'll get a confirmation email soon. The rest of your items are now ready to checkout.
