Wizard steppers portray progress through a sequence of steps.

Default Wizard Stepper

The default wizard stepper has horizontally aligned steps. The wizard stepper fills all available space and evenly distributes its steps.

Note that we have transition* for each of the steps. This will let the outgoing transition wizard step to match its color with the next wizard step state.
