A page notice appears prominently at the top of the page for high priority use cases; often including one or more calls to action.

To aid discoverabilty of such prominent content for assistive technology, we ensure that each page notice is a labelled, landmark region. Every page notice region begins with a short heading reflecting the visual status.

Single-Line Page Notice

A page notice may be a single, short block of heading text. A section of the text can be highlighted if desired.

Congrats: You just listed "Spam and Eggs From the Cow's Perspective (paperback)."

Error: Card number, Expiration date & Security code.

Hooray: free shipping on your next pair of shoes! Learn more.

Multi-Line Page Notice

The title can be moved to a separate block of heading text, creating a multi-line page notice. Apply the page-notice__title--multiline modifier to the title element.

Your new listing is live:

Spam and Eggs From the Cow's Perspective (paperback).

Please check the following details:

Card number, Expiration date & Security code.

Free shipping on your next pair of shoes:

Learn more.

Celebration Page Notice

A celebration-level page notice is always multiline.

Your order's in!

We'll email updates to jonsnow@gmail.com. You should get it by Thu, Sept 22.
