There are 3 types of tooltips available: traditional tooltips, infotips, and tourtips

Tooltips pointer can be positioned using any one of the following elements associated with respective type: *__arrow-top-left, *__arrow-top-right, *__arrow-top-center, *__arrow-bottom-top, *__arrow-bottom-right, *__arrow-bottom-center, *__arrow-left-top, *__arrow-left-bottom, *__arrow-left-center, *__arrow-right-top, *__arrow-right-center, *__arrow-right-bottom,

Tooltips

Traditional tooltips are used to label an icon, image, or other design element and are meant to be short and succint.

Infotips

Infotips activated when engaged by the user. Tapping or clicking the info bubble initiates flyout. Repeating the action closes the flyout, as does the "x". On large screens, the infotip is presented as a flyout. On small screens its a modal. Infotips are always white.
