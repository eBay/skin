There are 3 types of tooltips available:

  • Tooltip
  • Infotip
  • Tourtip

Tooltips pointer can be positioned using any one of the following elements based on the type:

  • arrow-top-left
  • arrow-top-right
  • arrow-top-center
  • arrow-bottom-top
  • arrow-bottom-right
  • arrow-bottom-center
  • arrow-left-top
  • arrow-left-bottom
  • arrow-left-center
  • arrow-right-top
  • arrow-right-center
  • arrow-right-bottom

Tooltip

Traditional tooltips are used to label an icon, image, or other design element and are meant to be short and succint.

Infotip

Infotips are activated when engaged by the user. Tapping or clicking the info bubble initiates flyout. Repeating the action closes the flyout, as does the "x". On large screens, the infotip is presented as a flyout. Infotips are always white.

Tourtip

As tourtips are meant to inform users of something new, they are often open by default. To close, the 'x' or a CTA is required. Tourtips are always blue.
